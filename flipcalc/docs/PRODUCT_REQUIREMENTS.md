# Product Requirements

## Product statement

FlipCalc helps resellers make better buying and selling decisions using purpose-built niche calculators rather than manually combining unrelated formulas.

## Target users

- Facebook Marketplace resellers
- eBay sellers
- Flea-market and estate-sale buyers
- Phone, tool, chainsaw, card, car-audio, and auto-parts flippers

## Core product principles

- Decision-focused outputs
- Transparent calculations
- No invented market prices
- User controls important assumptions
- Fast mobile operation
- Offline capability
- No account required for the initial version

## Planned calculator groups

### General

- General Flip Decision
- Maximum Buy Price
- Minimum Acceptable Offer
- Local Sale vs Shipped Sale

### Repair

- Repair vs Sell As-Is
- Part Out vs Sell Complete
- Untested Item Risk

### Niche

- Phone Flip
- Power Tool Flip
- Chainsaw Flip
- Trading Card Lot
- Car Audio Flip
- Auto Parts Flip

## Initial release

The first release will include the application foundation and a small set of fully tested calculators. Calculators must be added one at a time from written specifications.
